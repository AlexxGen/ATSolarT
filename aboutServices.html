Our Services

We specialize in providing comprehensive services for commercial photovoltaic (PV) solar systems. Our core offerings include Operations & Maintenance (O&M) agreements and performance monitoring to ensure systems run efficiently. We also handle reactive troubleshooting for faults in panel arrays and inverters, and perform repairs or equipment replacements (repowering) as needed to restore and maintain optimal system performance.

Know the Difference

Preventative Maintenance

Daily Monitoring:

  • Ensure the inverters are running in and producing properly. Notify the contact personnel of system faults and warnings
  • Ensure the firmware is updated to the current version
Annual Maintenance:
  • Ensure all the strings of solar panels are functioning as designed
  • Check for blown fuses and replace
  • All equipment with springs will be exercised to prevent spring compression damage (i.e. breakers and disconnects)
  • Annual O&M will be supported with a report containing the technical data to evaluate the PV systems performance and condition of the mechanicals like rust and structural damages
  • Check and clean inverter filters

Reactive Repairs

If during the maintenance and monitoring At Solar T identifies issues that will require Reactive Repairs, the repair you will be notified withing 48 hours and you have the option to dispatch us to the site. Reactive Repairs are charged separately on a case by case basis.

Beyond Maintenance

We also design and orchestrate the construction of new systems.
